Day 2 Detail

Lower Manhattan + Brooklyn Golden Hour

Waterfront-heavy day with smart timing for lower cost and better views.

Back to main itinerary

Morning Plan (8:00am-12:30pm)

  • Start at Battery Park early for ferry options and shorter security lines.
  • Budget route: Staten Island Ferry gives skyline views with no ticket cost.
  • Grab a quick breakfast in FiDi before museum or memorial stop.

Afternoon Plan (12:30pm-5:30pm)

  • Walk to 9/11 Memorial, then Wall Street and Trinity area.
  • Lunch around Seaport; weekday lunch specials are usually best value.
  • Cross to Brooklyn in late afternoon to catch softer skyline light.

Cheap But Amazing Date-Night

  • Walk Brooklyn Bridge around sunset, then split pizza in DUMBO.
  • Bring a dessert stop to Brooklyn Bridge Park for skyline night views.
  • Alternative budget date: ferry back + LES dumplings and a short night walk.

NYC Insider Info (Day 2)

  • Brooklyn Bridge pedestrian path gets packed by 7:00pm; go earlier when possible.
  • Use Citymapper or Google transit for live disruption alerts downtown.
  • DUMBO food near waterfront can be pricey; go a few blocks inland for better value.
