Day 3 Detail

Museum + Village + West Side Sunset

Flexible culture day with low-friction transit and a strong last-night finish.

Back to main itinerary

Morning Plan (9:00am-12:30pm)

  • Pick one major museum (The Met or MoMA) and pre-book timed entry.
  • Limit to 2-3 wings/exhibitions so you keep energy for afternoon walking.
  • Use museum cafes only for a quick break; lunch nearby is usually cheaper.

Afternoon Plan (12:30pm-5:30pm)

  • Head downtown to Greenwich Village for lunch and side streets.
  • Walk SoHo and Washington Square Park, then move west toward High Line.
  • Pause for coffee before sunset to avoid expensive rooftop drink spend.

Cheap But Amazing Date-Night

  • Sunset walk on the High Line, then split plates in Chelsea Market area.
  • Optional upgrade: one paid observation deck, but skip paid drinks there.
  • Best low-cost finish: riverside walk with bakery dessert instead of lounge bars.

NYC Insider Info (Day 3)

  • Museum gift shops can consume time fast; set a 10-minute cap.
  • West Village and SoHo get crowded on weekends after 2:00pm.
  • For faster subway transfers, stand near the middle cars on most Manhattan lines.
