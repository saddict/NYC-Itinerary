Meredith Purdy's 3-Day NYC Itinerary

Day 1

Midtown + Central Park + Broadway

  • Morning: Breakfast near Bryant Park, visit NY Public Library, then stroll 5th Avenue. Map
  • Afternoon: Central Park loop (Bethesda Terrace, Bow Bridge), quick lunch on Upper West Side. Map
  • Evening: Times Square at sunset and a Broadway show. Map

Day 2

Lower Manhattan + Brooklyn Views

  • Morning: Statue of Liberty/Ellis Island ferry or Staten Island Ferry for budget option. Map
  • Afternoon: 9/11 Memorial, Wall Street, then lunch in Seaport. Map
  • Evening: Walk Brooklyn Bridge, dinner in DUMBO, skyline photos at Brooklyn Bridge Park. Map

Day 3

Museums + Village + Skyline

  • Morning: Choose one: The Met or MoMA. Map
  • Afternoon: Lunch in Greenwich Village, explore SoHo + Washington Square Park. Map
  • Evening: High Line walk and sunset at Hudson Yards or Top of the Rock. Map

Budget + Transit Tips

  • Use contactless tap-to-pay for subway/buses (OMNY).
  • Book skyline attractions and Broadway tickets in advance.
  • Wear walking shoes; this plan averages 18k-24k steps/day.
