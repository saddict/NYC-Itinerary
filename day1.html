Midtown + Central Park + Theater Night

Built for Meredith and Adi with practical timing and local strategy.

Morning Plan (8:00am-12:00pm)

  • 8:00am breakfast near Bryant Park; arrive before 9:00am rush.
  • 9:30am NY Public Library main hall, then 5th Avenue window walk.
  • 11:15am quick coffee before entering Central Park south side.

Afternoon Plan (12:00pm-5:30pm)

  • Central Park loop: Bethesda Terrace, Bow Bridge, Sheep Meadow.
  • Budget lunch on Upper West Side to avoid Midtown markups.
  • Late afternoon break back at hotel before evening crowds.

Cheap But Amazing Date-Night

  • Do Times Square photos first, then buy same-day discount Broadway tickets at TKTS.
  • If shows are expensive, swap to off-Broadway + late dessert in Hell's Kitchen.
  • Optional low-cost romantic add-on: night walk at Bryant Park after the show.

NYC Insider Info (Day 1)

  • Walk 2-3 avenues east/west for better food prices than Broadway-adjacent spots.
  • Central Park bathrooms and water points are easier around major landmarks.
  • For Broadway, weeknights usually have better seat availability than Friday/Saturday.
